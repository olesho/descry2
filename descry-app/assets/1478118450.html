    Gorgeous Fabric!
    By Rebecca Taylor from Maryland on Feb 11, 2015
    Casual Do-It-Yourselfer
    The fabric on this chair is gorgeous. When I removed the chair from the box, the first thing I noticed was how the lighting accented the subtle iridescent sheen in the design of the fabric. It really catches the eye! The nail head trim is the perfect finishing touch, too.. The chair was delivered in a well packaged box and assembly took less than 15 minutes because only the legs needed to be attached. I originally bought this chair for my bedroom, but the gorgeous fabric inspired me to place it in another room where it would be the dominant furniture. So, now I am ordering a 2nd one of these chairs to pair with a table.
